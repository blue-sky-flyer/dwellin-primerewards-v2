How Dwellin Works

Simple, step-by-step process to track, earn, and protect.

Step 1: Track Your Maintenance

Confirm when you complete maintenance tasks in the Dwellin app or website. You can track tasks in two ways:

📝 Self-Report

Quick check-ins for routine tasks like lawn mowing, air filter changes, and gutter cleaning. No receipts needed—just confirm you did it.

📸 Upload Receipt

For major services, upload a photo of your receipt or invoice. Proof needed for reimbursement eligibility.

📋

Track Everything in One Place

No scattered receipts. No lost documentation. Everything organized and searchable.

Step 2: Earn Rewards

Based on what you tracked, you earn in two ways:

💰 Reimbursements (Prime Only)

$75–$150 per service for annual/bi-annual services like HVAC, roof inspection, water heater flushing, and plumbing checks.

⭐ Points (Prime Only)

5–25 points for routine tasks. Accumulate points toward exclusive rewards and benefits.

💸

Get Paid for Maintenance

Reimbursements process within 3–5 business days to your account.

Typical Annual Earnings: $300–$425+

Step 3: Protect Your Home

Every task you track builds a comprehensive maintenance history. This documentation protects you in important ways:

🛡️ Prove Maintenance

When you file an insurance claim, comprehensive records prove you maintained your home properly. This accelerates approvals.

🏠 Reduce Risk

Regular maintenance prevents emergencies. Know what's coming due and address issues before they become disasters.

📜 10-Year History

Keep 10 years of home records. Useful for home sales, refinancing, and long-term asset protection.

🔒

Insurance-Ready Documentation

When you need to file a claim, share your records directly with insurers. Faster approvals. Better outcomes.

Step 4: Optimize & Improve

Over time, Dwellin learns your home and gives you intelligence to maintain it better:

📊 Home Health Score

Visual dashboard showing your home's maintenance status and what's coming up next.

🎯 Expert Guidance

Learn what maintenance your specific home needs, when to do it, and why it matters.

📅 Seasonal Planning

Tailored guidance for spring, summer, fall, and winter maintenance. Never miss a seasonal task.

📈

Continuous Improvement

The more you use Dwellin, the smarter it gets about your home and what it needs.

Common Questions About the Process

How long does it take to get reimbursed?

Most reimbursements process within 3–5 business days of approval. If you use a partner contractor, reimbursement is even faster (sometimes same-day).

Can I use any contractor?

Yes! Upload receipts from any licensed service provider. We also have a preferred partner network that processes reimbursements faster.

What counts as a reimbursable service?

Services must be performed by licensed professionals and include: HVAC service, roof inspection, water heater flushing, and plumbing system checks. All other services are routine (self-reported) and earn points.

Is my home data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely on our servers. You have full control over what information is shared with insurers and other parties.

Can I use Dwellin on mobile and web?

Yes. The Dwellin app works on iOS and Android. You can also access your account on web via our dashboard. All data syncs across devices.
