How Dwellin Works

4 Simple Steps to Better Home Maintenance

1

Track Maintenance

Log when you complete home maintenance tasks. Upload receipts for major services or self-report routine tasks. Everything is stored securely in one place.

2

Earn Rewards

Get reimbursed for annual services ($75-$150 each) with Dwellin Prime. Earn points on routine tasks (5-25 points each). Accumulate benefits as you maintain your home.

3

Build Protection

Create a comprehensive 10-year maintenance history. When you need to file an insurance claim, you have documented proof that your home was properly maintained.

4

Optimize Care

View your home health score. Get reminders about what maintenance is due next. Receive expert guidance on caring for your most valuable asset.

Well-maintained homes hold their value better. Documentation proves you cared for your investment properly.
