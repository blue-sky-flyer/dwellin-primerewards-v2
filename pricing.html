Pricing & Billing Questions

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. Switch from monthly to annual (or vice versa) anytime. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What happens if I cancel?

You'll lose access to Prime features and reimbursements. However, your home records remain available in your free account.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price you see is the price you pay. No setup fees, no surprise charges. Full transparency always.

Do reimbursements work on both plans?

Yes. Both annual and monthly Prime members get the same reimbursement benefits. The only difference is billing frequency.

Is there a free trial?

Start with our Free tier to explore Dwellin. When you're ready, upgrade to Prime for full benefits. No credit card required to get started.
