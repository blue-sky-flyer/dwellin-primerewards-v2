Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. Dwellin Prime members receive priority support and typically hear back within 2-4 hours during business hours.

I'm already a Dwellin user. Where's my account support?

For account-related questions, billing, or technical issues, please use the in-app support feature or contact support@dwellin.com directly. Provide your account email for faster resolution.

I have a suggestion or feedback

We love hearing from our community! Send us your ideas using the form above with "Feedback" or "Feature Request" in the subject line. Your input helps shape Dwellin's future.

Are you hiring?

Yes! We're always looking for talented people to join our team. If you're interested, email us at careers@dwellin.com or visit our About page for current opportunities.

Is there a phone number I can call?

We primarily communicate via email to ensure proper documentation and support. For urgent issues, please email support@dwellin.com and mark as "URGENT" in the subject line.
