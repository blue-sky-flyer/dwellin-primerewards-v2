Learn What Your Home Needs

Expert guidance on maintenance tasks and how to get rewarded for doing them.

Educational guides from industry experts. Learn what to do, when to do it, and how to earn rewards while maintaining your home. Each article is tied to reimbursements or points—join Dwellin Prime to unlock the full value.

💰 Major Services (Get Reimbursed)

These annual or bi-annual services are reimbursable with Dwellin Prime ($75–$150 each).

🌬️

HVAC System Service

💵 $100 Reimbursement

Frequency: Annual (ideally before summer cooling season)

Regular HVAC maintenance keeps your system running efficiently and extends its lifespan. During service, technicians clean filters, check refrigerant levels, and inspect components.

Why it matters: A well-maintained HVAC system prevents emergency breakdowns, keeps energy costs down, and avoids expensive repairs.

Learn More & Get Reimbursed →
🏠

Roof Inspection

💵 $150 Reimbursement

Frequency: Annual (spring or fall)

Professional roof inspection catches small problems before they become expensive water damage inside your home. Inspectors look for missing shingles, flashing issues, and potential leaks.

Why it matters: Roof issues aren't always visible from the ground. Annual inspections prevent water damage that can cost thousands to repair.

Learn More & Get Reimbursed →
💧

Water Heater Maintenance

💵 $75 Reimbursement

Frequency: Annual

Mineral deposits accumulate in water heaters over time, reducing efficiency and shortening lifespan. Flushing removes these deposits and keeps the system running optimally.

Why it matters: This simple maintenance task can extend your water heater life by 5+ years. That's thousands of dollars saved compared to early replacement.

Learn More & Get Reimbursed →
🔧

Plumbing System Check

💵 $100 Reimbursement

Frequency: Every 2 years

Professional plumbing inspection identifies small leaks, water pressure issues, and potential problems before they become expensive emergencies. Plumbers check visible pipes, water pressure, and system integrity.

Why it matters: Small leaks hidden behind walls can cause foundation issues and mold. Catching them early saves thousands in remediation.

Learn More & Get Reimbursed →

⭐ Routine Tasks (Earn Points)

These routine tasks earn you 5–25 points per completion with Dwellin Prime. Points accumulate toward exclusive rewards.

🌬️

Air Filter Replacement

⭐ 10 Points

Frequency: Monthly

Clean air filters improve HVAC system efficiency and indoor air quality. This is a quick DIY task that makes a big difference.

Tip: Stock extra filters at home so you never skip a month.

Log in App & Earn Points →
🌊

Gutter Cleaning

⭐ 25 Points

Frequency: Quarterly

Leaves and debris clog gutters, causing water to overflow and damage your roof, siding, and foundation. Regular cleaning prevents costly damage.

Tip: Install gutter guards to reduce cleaning frequency and prevent future debris buildup.

Log in App & Earn Points →
🌱

Lawn Mowing

⭐ 15 Points

Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly during growing season

Regular mowing keeps grass healthy, improves curb appeal, and prevents overgrowth that attracts pests. A well-maintained lawn is the first thing people see.

Tip: Never cut more than 1/3 of the grass blade height at once to keep grass healthy.

Log in App & Earn Points →
🚨

Smoke Detector Testing

⭐ 5 Points

Frequency: Monthly

Smoke detectors save lives. Monthly testing ensures they're working when you need them most. It takes 30 seconds but could save everything.

Tip: Replace batteries annually even if detectors are working. Set a phone reminder for the first of every month.
