About Dwellin

We believe homes should be simple to maintain, rewarding to care for, and always protected.

Our Story

Dwellin was founded on a simple observation: homeowners are doing their best to maintain their homes, but they're doing it alone. They don't know what maintenance is needed, when to do it, or why it matters. And when they spend time and money maintaining their homes—that effort goes unrecognized.

We started Dwellin to change that. We believe homeownership should be empowering, not overwhelming. That maintenance should be rewarding, not a burden. That documentation should be automatic, not a scramble when insurance claims happen.

Today, thousands of homeowners use Dwellin to track maintenance, earn rewards, and protect their most valuable asset.

Our Mission

To make home maintenance simple, rewarding, and protective—so homeowners can spend less time worrying about their homes and more time enjoying them.

Our Values

Simplicity

Maintenance shouldn't be complicated. We remove friction from home maintenance and documentation.

Transparency

We're upfront about pricing and features. No hidden costs. What you see is what you get.

Empowerment

Homeowners should feel confident and in control. We give them the tools and knowledge they need.
