About Dwellin

We believe homes should be simple to maintain, rewarding to care for, and always protected.

Our Story

Dwellin was founded on a simple observation: homeowners are doing their best to take care of their homes, but they're doing it alone. They don't know what maintenance they need, when to do it, or why it matters. And when they do maintain their homes—spending time and money—that effort goes unrecognized.

We started Dwellin to change that. We believe that homeownership should be empowering, not overwhelming. That maintenance should be rewarding, not a burden. That documentation should be automatic, not a scramble when insurance claims hit.

Today, thousands of homeowners use Dwellin to track maintenance, earn rewards, and protect their most valuable asset: their home.

Our Mission

To make home maintenance simple, rewarding, and protective—so homeowners can spend less time worrying about their homes and more time enjoying them.

📚

Educate

Teach homeowners what maintenance their home actually needs, when to do it, and why it matters for long-term protection.

💰

Reward

Give homeowners financial incentives for doing the right maintenance. Earn reimbursements and points for taking care of their homes.

🛡️

Protect

Build comprehensive documentation that protects homeowners when insurance claims arise and proves their homes were well-maintained.

Our Values

Simplicity

We make complexity simple. Maintenance tracking shouldn't require a PhD. Documentation shouldn't be stressful. Dwellin removes friction from home maintenance.

Transparency

We're upfront about pricing, features, and what homeowners get. No hidden fees. No surprise limitations. What you see is what you get.

Empowerment

We believe homeowners should feel confident and in control. Dwellin gives them the knowledge, tools, and rewards to maintain their homes with confidence.

Reliability

Your home documentation is critical. We secure it with encryption, back it up reliably, and ensure it's available when you need it.

Responsiveness

When Dwellin Prime members have questions, we respond fast. Our support team is here to help, not to hide behind automated systems.

Impact

We measure success by homeowner outcomes. Are homes being maintained better? Are insurance claims being processed faster? Are homeowners sleeping better at night?
