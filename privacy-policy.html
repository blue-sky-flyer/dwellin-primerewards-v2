Privacy Policy for Dwellin

At Dwellin ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Create or update your Dwellin account
  • Add home information and maintenance history
  • Upload receipts or photos of receipts
  • Submit support requests or feedback
  • Make purchases through Dwellin Prime
  • Communicate with us via email or through our app

This information may include: name, email address, phone number, home address, home details, maintenance records, photos, payment information, and any other information you choose to share.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, frequency, duration of use)
  • Location information (if you permit the app to access it)
  • Log data (timestamps, IP addresses, referring URLs)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Services
  • Process your transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices and support communications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Personalize your experience with Dwellin
  • Send marketing and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Payment processors (to process Dwellin Prime subscriptions)
  • Cloud storage providers (to securely store your maintenance records)
  • Analytics providers (to understand how you use our Services)
  • Customer support platforms

With Your Permission

You can authorize us to share your maintenance records with insurance companies or other parties to support insurance claims. You always control what information is shared and with whom.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Protect the safety, rights, and property of Dwellin, our users, or the public

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Limited employee access to personal information
  • Confidentiality agreements with our service providers

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Portability

You have the right to access your personal information and request a copy of your data in a portable format.

Correction and Deletion

You can update or correct your account information at any time through your Dwellin account settings. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@dwellin.com.

Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or adjusting your notification settings in the app.

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our Services do not respond to Do Not Track signals at this time.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You can request deletion of your account at any time. Some information may be retained for legal, accounting, or operational purposes after account deletion.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and maintained in, computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ. If you are located outside the United States and choose to use our Services, you acknowledge that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of the sale of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@dwellin.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes via email or through a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of our Services following such notification constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
