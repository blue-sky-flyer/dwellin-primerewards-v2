Privacy Policy for Dwellin

At Dwellin, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Home information (address, home type, systems)
  • Maintenance records (receipts, photos, dates)
  • Payment information (for Dwellin Prime)
  • Contact information and support messages

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (type, OS, unique identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, frequency)
  • Log data (timestamps, IP addresses)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Services
  • Process transactions and send receipts
  • Send technical notices and support
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Personalize your experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal information
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular backups

Your Rights

Access and Portability

You have the right to access your personal information and request a portable copy of your data.

Correction and Deletion

You can update or delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting support@dwellin.com.

Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in our messages.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for payment processing, analytics, and cloud hosting. All service providers comply with our security and privacy standards.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be announced via email or prominent notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have privacy questions or concerns, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@dwellin.com
